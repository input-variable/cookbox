Spiced Turnips with spinach and tomatoes

Preperation time: 15 mins

Cooking time: 8 mins

Serves: 6

Sweet baby turnips, lender spinach and ripe tomatoes make tempting partners in this simple Eastern Mediterranean vegetable stew.

Ingredients

  • Plum or other well-flavoured tomatoes (450g/1lb)
  • Olive oil (60ml/4 tbsp.)
  • Onions, sliced (x2)
  • Baby turnips, peeled (450g/1lb)
  • Paprika (5ml/1 tsp.)
  • Caster Sugar (2.5ml / half a tsp.)
  • Fresh Coriander, chopped (60ml/4 tbsp.)
  • Fresh Young Spinach, stalks removed (450g/1lb)
  • Salt and Ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Plunge the tomatoes into a bowl of boiling water for 30 seconds, then refresh in a bowl of cold water. Peel away the tomato skins and chop roughly. Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan or saut√© pan and fry the onion slices for about 5 minutes until golden.
  2. Add the baby turnips, tomatoes and paprika to the pan with 60ml/4 tbsp. water and cook until the tomatoes are pulpy. Cover with a lid and continue cooking until the baby turnips have softened.
  3. Stir in the sugar and coriander, then add the spinach and a little salt and pepper and cook for a further 2-3 minutes until the spinach has wilted. Serve warm or cold.
